How do I upload a digital exam?

To book an exam in South Africa, you need to follow these steps:

  1. Go to the website https://trinityregistration.azurewebsites.net/
  2. If you haven't used the site before, register. If you have, login.
  3. Fill in all your contact details on the registration page, including the postal code.
  4. Make sure to select the correct South Africa representative or area.
  5. Click on the appropriate registration option if it is available.

Please note that if you are registering for a digital exam, there may be a delay of up to five days before you receive an email asking you to upload your exam recording.
